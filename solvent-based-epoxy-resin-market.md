<p><p>The solvent-based epoxy resin market can be categorized into two main types: direct and indirect processes. The direct process involves mixing epoxy resin with solvents to create a ready-to-use product, ideal for quick applications and faster curing times. In contrast, the indirect process focuses on producing epoxy resins through a series of chemical reactions, resulting in more complex formulations and enhanced properties. Both methods cater to various industrial applications, including coatings, adhesives, and composite manufacturing.</p></p>
