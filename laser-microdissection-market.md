UV LMD utilizes a focused ultraviolet laser to isolate and extract specific cell types or tissue sections from samples, ensuring minimal damage to the surrounding material. In contrast, IR LMD employs infrared lasers to achieve similar results, often suitable for thicker samples. Both techniques play crucial roles in fields like genomics, proteomics, and cancer research, enabling precise analysis and study of targeted biological materials.</p></p>
