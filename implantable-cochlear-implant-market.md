<p><strong>The Implantable Cochlear Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monaural</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable cochlear implant market is divided into two primary types: monaural and binaural. Monaural implants are designed for use in one ear, catering to individuals with unilateral hearing loss, while binaural implants are fitted in both ears, providing a more natural hearing experience and improved sound localization for those with bilateral hearing impairment. The choice between monaural and binaural options depends on the degree and type of hearing loss, as well as patient preferences for auditory support.</p></p>
