<p><ul><li>Transdermal Drug Delivery Devices</li><li>Topical Drug Delivery Formulations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The topical drug delivery market encompasses two primary types: transdermal drug delivery devices and topical drug delivery formulations. Transdermal devices, such as patches, facilitate the absorption of drugs through the skin into the bloodstream, providing sustained systemic effects. On the other hand, topical formulations, including creams, ointments, and gels, deliver medication directly to localized skin areas, targeting specific conditions. Both types aim to enhance therapeutic effectiveness while minimizing systemic side effects and improving patient compliance.</p></p>
