<p><ul><li>Flail Mower</li><li>Boom Mower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mounted Reach Mower market includes two primary types: Flail Mowers and Boom Mowers. Flail Mowers utilize multiple small blades attached to a rotating drum, allowing for effective cutting of dense vegetation and grass. They are ideal for maintaining larger landscapes and fields. Boom Mowers, on the other hand, feature an extendable arm that allows for mowing in hard-to-reach areas, such as ditches and embankments, providing versatility for roadside and agricultural maintenance. Both types enhance efficiency in land management.</p></p>
