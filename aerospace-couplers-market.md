<p><ul><li>Emergency Breakaway Coupler</li><li>Pressure Coupler</li><li>Hydrant Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace couplers market features several key types designed for specific fueling applications. Emergency breakaway couplers are safety devices that disconnect under stress, preventing fuel spillages during accidents. Pressure couplers are designed to maintain pressure while ensuring a secure connection between fuel sources and aircraft. Hydrant couplers facilitate the transfer of fuel from hydrant systems to aircraft, enabling efficient ground refueling operations. Together, these couplers enhance safety, efficiency, and reliability in aviation fuel management.</p></p>
