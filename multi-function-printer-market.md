<p><p>The multi-function printer market encompasses various types, primarily laser and inkjet printers. In contrast, inkjet printers spray liquid ink onto paper, providing vibrant color and superior photo quality, suited for home users and small businesses. Both types offer scanning, copying, and faxing capabilities, allowing users to meet diverse printing needs in a single device.</p></p>
