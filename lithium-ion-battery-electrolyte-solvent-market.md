<p><p>The lithium-ion battery electrolyte solvent market comprises various solvents essential for optimal battery performance. Ethylene Carbonate (EC) acts as a polar solvent enhancing conductivity. Diethyl Carbonate (DEC) offers good solvating properties and low viscosity. Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC) provides stability and is environmentally friendly. Ethyl Methyl Carbonate (EMC) enhances energy density and thermal stability. Propylene Carbonate (PC) is known for its high dielectric constant. Other solvents may include various blends aimed at improving efficiency and battery life.</p></p>
