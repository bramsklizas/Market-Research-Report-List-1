<p><p>The Lacrimal Duct Stent System Market comprises various types of stents, primarily categorized into Mono Canalicular Stents and Canalicular Stents. Mono Canalicular Stents are designed to be placed in a single canaliculus, facilitating tear drainage in cases of obstruction. In contrast, Canalicular Stents encompass a wider design that accommodates both superior and inferior canaliculi, enhancing tear flow and offering more comprehensive management of lacrimal drainage issues. Both types are essential for treating conditions like epiphora and post-surgical recovery.</p></p>
