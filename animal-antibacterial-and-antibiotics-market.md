<p><p>The animal antibacterial and antibiotics market comprises several key categories, each serving specific therapeutic needs. Sulfonamides target bacterial growth by inhibiting folic acid synthesis. Macrolides offer effectiveness against respiratory and soft tissue infections. Aminoglycosides are utilized for severe infections due to their potency, while cephalosporins provide a diverse range of antibiotic activity, making them crucial in veterinary medicine for treating infections in animals.</p></p>
