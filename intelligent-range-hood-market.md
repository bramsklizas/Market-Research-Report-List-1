<p><ul><li>Side Suction</li><li>Direct Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent range hood market is primarily divided into two types: side suction and direct suction. Side suction range hoods are designed to draw air from the sides, effectively capturing fumes and grease while allowing for a sleek, integrated kitchen design. In contrast, direct suction models feature a vertical exhaust system that pulls air directly above the cooking surface, providing efficient ventilation. Both types aim to enhance cooking experiences by improving air quality and minimizing odors in modern kitchens.</p></p>
