<p><p>Audience Response Software (ARS) can be categorized into two main types: web-based and installed markets. Web-based ARS operates through online platforms, allowing participants to respond using their devices without needing specific software installation. This flexibility enhances accessibility and ease of use. In contrast, installed ARS requires users to download and install software on individual devices. While it may provide more robust features and greater control, the installation process can create barriers for participation. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
