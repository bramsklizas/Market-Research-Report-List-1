<p><p>The food processing spiral freezers market includes two main types: single drum and double drum spiral freezers. Single drum spiral freezers utilize a single helical conveyor that circulates products through a cooling chamber, providing efficient freezing for various foods. Double drum spiral freezers, on the other hand, feature two interwoven conveyors, allowing for higher throughput and enhanced freezing capabilities. Both types are designed to optimize space and energy efficiency while maintaining product quality during the freezing process.</p></p>
