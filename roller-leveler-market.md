<p><ul><li>Two-roll</li><li>Multi-roll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roller leveler market is primarily segmented into two types: two-roll and multi-roll systems. Two-roll levelers utilize a pair of rollers to flatten metal sheets, ideal for simpler tasks and smaller operations. In contrast, multi-roll levelers feature multiple rollers arranged in a series, providing more precise leveling and handling of thicker or wider materials. Multi-roll systems are often favored for high-volume production settings where superior flatness and efficiency are essential, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
