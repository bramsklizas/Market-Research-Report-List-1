<p><ul><li>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID)</li><li>Real Time Locating Systems (RTLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Container tracking and security market encompasses technologies like Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Real Time Locating Systems (RTLS). RFID uses radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to containers, enhancing inventory management and security. In contrast, RTLS employs various technologies, including GPS and infrared, to provide real-time location data of containers within facilities or on routes. Both systems improve logistics efficiency, reduce loss, and ensure the security of cargo throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
