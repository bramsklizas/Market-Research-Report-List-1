<p><p>A Vortex Flow Controller is an advanced instrument utilized for measuring and regulating the flow of fluids, both liquids and gases, based on the vortex shedding principle. As fluid flows past a bluff body within the device, vortices are generated, which can be detected and measured to determine flow rates.
