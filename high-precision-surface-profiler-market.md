In contrast, 3D surface metrology offers a comprehensive spatial analysis, mapping surface features in three dimensions. This allows for a more in-depth understanding of surface profiles, essential for industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, where precision is critical.</p></p>
