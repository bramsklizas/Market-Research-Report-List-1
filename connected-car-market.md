<p><ul><li>Embedded solutions</li><li>Integrated solutions</li><li>Tethered solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The connected car market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Embedded solutions** involve built-in connectivity features within the vehicle, allowing seamless communication with external networks without relying on external devices. </p><p>2. **Integrated solutions** combine embedded systems with smartphone connectivity, enabling a hybrid approach where both in-car technology and mobile devices work together for enhanced functionality. </p><p>3. **Tethered solutions** depend on a smartphone's connection to provide internet access, relying on the mobile device for data transmission and services, rather than having built-in capabilities.</p></p>
