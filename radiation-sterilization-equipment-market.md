<p><strong>The Radiation Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-Beam Radiation Sterilization</li><li>X-Ray Sterilization</li><li>Gamma Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation sterilization equipment market comprises three main types: E-Beam Radiation Sterilization, X-Ray Sterilization, and Gamma Sterilization. E-Beam utilizes high-energy electrons for rapid sterilization, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. X-Ray Sterilization employs high-energy X-rays to penetrate and sterilize various products, offering flexibility in processing. Gamma Sterilization utilizes gamma rays from radioactive isotopes, effectively sterilizing large volumes of materials and widely used in medical and pharmaceutical applications. Each method serves distinct needs in ensuring the safety and efficacy of products.</p></p>
