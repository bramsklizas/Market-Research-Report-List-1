<p><p>Above knee prosthetics, also known as transfemoral prosthetics, are artificial limbs designed for individuals who have lost a leg above the knee due to injury, disease, or congenital conditions. Additionally, the integration of smart technology and biomechanical sensors into prosthetic devices is allowing for more personalized and functional solutions tailored to users' needs. The growing geriatric population and rising incidence of conditions leading to amputations, such as diabetes and vascular diseases, further stimulate demand.
