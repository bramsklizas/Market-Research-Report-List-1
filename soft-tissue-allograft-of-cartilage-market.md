<p><p>The soft tissue allograft cartilage market is categorized into three main types: hyaline cartilage, elastic cartilage, and fibrocartilage. Hyaline cartilage is predominantly used in joint repair and reconstruction due to its smooth surface and ability to support load. Elastic cartilage provides flexibility, making it suitable for structures like the ear. Fibrocartilage, characterized by its tough and dense nature, is essential for intervertebral discs and menisci. Each type serves specific clinical needs, influencing demand and application in orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
