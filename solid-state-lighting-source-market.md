<p><p>The solid-state lighting source market encompasses three primary types: Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs), Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLEDs), and Polymer Light Emitting Diodes (PLEDs). OLEDs offer flexibility and superior color quality, suitable for displays and decorative lighting. PLEDs, a subset of OLEDs, utilize polymers for illumination, enabling lightweight and cost-effective solutions in consumer electronics and lighting designs, reinforcing the shift toward sustainable energy applications.</p></p>
