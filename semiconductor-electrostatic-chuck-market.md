<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor electrostatic chuck market comprises two main types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type electrostatic chucks utilize electric fields to generate attractive forces that hold wafers in place, making them suitable for various semiconductor processes. In contrast, JR Type electrostatic chucks leverage a specialized mechanism involving an insulating layer combined with electrodes to achieve a more uniform and controllable force distribution, enhancing performance for advanced applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Both types are essential for ensuring precise wafer handling and processing.</p></p>
