<p><p>Semiconductor metrology and inspection equipment are crucial for ensuring the precision and quality of semiconductor manufacturing. Optical metrology utilizes light-based techniques to measure critical dimensions and surface characteristics, offering fast and non-destructive evaluations. E-beam (electron beam) metrology, on the other hand, employs focused electrons for high-resolution inspections, allowing for more detailed analyses of nanoscale features. Both types are integral in enhancing process control and achieving the stringent tolerances required in modern semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
