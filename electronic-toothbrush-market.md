<p><p>The electronic toothbrush market features two main types: vibration motion and rotation-oscillation motion. Vibration motion toothbrushes utilize sonic technology, generating rapid vibrations to dislodge plaque while promoting blood circulation in the gums. In contrast, rotation-oscillation motion toothbrushes employ a round brush head that rotates back and forth, effectively cleaning each tooth surface and removing debris. Both types offer enhanced cleaning capabilities compared to manual brushing, contributing to improved oral hygiene and catering to varying consumer preferences.</p></p>
