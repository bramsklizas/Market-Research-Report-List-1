<p><p>The Medical Monitors and Displays market includes various technologies such as TFT-LCD, IPS-LCD, OLED, and others. TFT-LCD (Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display) offers good image quality and is cost-effective, commonly used in patient monitoring. IPS-LCD (In-Plane Switching Liquid Crystal Display) provides better color accuracy and wide viewing angles, ideal for diagnostic imaging. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers superior contrast and faster response times, enhancing visual clarity. Other emerging technologies continue to develop, catering to specific medical imaging needs.</p></p>
