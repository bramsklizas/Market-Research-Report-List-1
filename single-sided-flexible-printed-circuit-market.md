<p><p>The Single Sided Flexible Printed Circuit (FPC) market is primarily divided into aluminum-based and copper-based types. Aluminum-based FPCs are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer excellent thermal conductivity, making them suitable for applications requiring heat dissipation. In contrast, copper-based FPCs are favored for their superior electrical conductivity and versatility, often used in high-performance electronics. Both types cater to distinct applications, with aluminum dominating in thermal management solutions and copper excelling in complex electronic devices requiring reliable signal transmission.</p></p>
