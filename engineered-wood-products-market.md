<p><p>The engineered wood products market consists of several key types. Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) is made from glued layers of wood veneer, providing superior strength for structural applications. Structural wood I-beams feature an I-shaped design for enhanced load-bearing capabilities, often used in floor and roof systems. Glued Laminated Timber (glulam) combines layers of timber, offering versatility in large spans for beams and columns. Additionally, other products include cross-laminated timber (CLT) and laminated strand lumber (LSL), catering to various construction needs.</p></p>
