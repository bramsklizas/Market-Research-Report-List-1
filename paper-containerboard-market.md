<p><strong>The Paper Containerboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kraftliner</li><li>Testliner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper containerboard market primarily consists of two types: Kraftliner and Testliner. Kraftliner is a strong, high-quality corrugated medium made from virgin fibers, primarily used for the outer layers of corrugated boxes, offering excellent durability and protection for heavy goods. Testliner, on the other hand, is produced from recycled paper and is used as a less expensive alternative for the inner liner of corrugated boxes, providing sufficient strength while promoting sustainability through recycled content.</p></p>
