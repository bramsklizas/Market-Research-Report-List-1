<p><p>The mobile power generation equipment market primarily comprises two types: internal combustion engines and electric generators. Internal combustion engines utilize fuel like diesel or gasoline to generate power, offering high energy output and portability, making them ideal for emergency backup and remote applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, events, and disaster relief.</p></p>
