<p><strong>The X-Ray Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Microscopes market is primarily divided into two types: Transmission X-Ray Microscopes (TXM) and Scanning X-Ray Microscopes (SXM). TXM utilizes transmitted X-rays to create high-resolution images of specimens, making it suitable for visualizing internal structures and materials at the nanoscale. In contrast, SXM employs focused X-rays that scan the sample surface, offering detailed topographical information. Both types are widely used in fields like materials science, biology, and semiconductor research, each serving unique imaging and analytical needs.</p></p>
